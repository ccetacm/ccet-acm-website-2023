CCET ACM Student Chapter is Recruiting

Dec. 18-21, 2021

CCET ACM Student Chapter believes in providing a healthy environment where creativity and imagination can flourish. Through collaboration and engagement in a plethora of technical activities and projects, we envision building a community of like-minded people who love to code, collaborate, and have fun! We at CASC are the platform where future programmers can multiply their potential and start a new programming generation.
